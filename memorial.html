BREACH 001 · 2019

The Supply Chain Night

On a quiet Tuesday, a signed update package bypassed every perimeter control. The SIEM saw nothing for seventy-two hours — the publisher was trusted, the hash was valid, and the attackers had studied our blind spots.

BREACH 002 · 2020

The Ransomware Pivot

BREACH 003 · 2021

Cloud Misconfiguration

BREACH 004 · 2022

Insider Exfiltration

BREACH 005 · 2023

API Key Harvest
