﻿# References

The security work leans on a handful of standard references: the OWASP Top 10 for the threat checklist, MITRE ATT&CK for technique mapping, and STRIDE for categorizing the rules. The data sources are MaxMind GeoLite2 for geolocation and AbuseIPDB and VirusTotal for IP reputation. Anything cited in the other pages traces back to one of these.
