﻿# EPS monitoring

Events per second is the headline number for any SIEM, so the Pipeline Health view tracks ingest rate over time. It's a handy sanity check during a demo: a simulated campaign should visibly bump the rate, and a flat line when logs should be flowing points at a stalled parser or ingest path. The figure is approximate, meant to give a feel for throughput rather than billing-grade accounting.
