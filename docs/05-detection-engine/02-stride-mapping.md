﻿# STRIDE mapping

Every rule maps to one STRIDE category, which is how the threat model and the rules line up. Spoofing covers brute-force and off-hours; Tampering covers sql-injection, xss, and file-tampering; Information Disclosure covers data-exfil and sensitive-path; Denial of Service covers rapid-requests; Elevation of Privilege covers priv-esc; Repudiation covers unusual-port. The mapping shows up in the MITRE Matrix and correlation views, so an analyst can pivot from a category to the rules under it.
