﻿# Reports

HABIBI-SIEM exposes this capability under **Reporting → Reports**. Reports generates exportable summaries of alert statistics and module highlights for coursework deliverables.

## What you see on screen

Pick template, time window, and format (PDF or CSV where available). Preview before download.

## How data moves through the dashboard

Report data snapshots current client statistics; scheduled runs may be described in Scheduler module.

## Day-to-day operator workflow

Export after Simulate Campaign for rich charts. Name files with date and scenario for grading clarity.

## Edge cases and false trails

Large exports with zero alerts produce empty shells; ingest first. Browser pop-up blockers can stop downloads silently.
